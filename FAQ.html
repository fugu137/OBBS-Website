Frequently Asked Questions

  1. 1. What is the difference between bookkeeping & accounting?

    Bookkeeping is about the day-in-day-out financial record keeping and reporting and accounting is using the financial information to make important financial decisions about a business entity.

  2. 2. Why should I outsource my bookkeeping?

    Outsourcing your bookkeeping allows you to focus on your business. It frees up your time to allow you to focus on revenue generating activities. Outsourced bookkeeping services can result in improved efficiencies, cost savings, access to financial expertise not available in-house, and an improvement in being able to monitor your business finances.

  3. 3. How often should I use a bookkeeper?

    This will depend on your or your businesses needs. If you are a sole trader, you may only require the services of a bookkeeper on a quarterly or monthly basis. Bookkeepers can prepare financial reports and statement on a daily, weekly, monthly or periodic basis.

  4. 4. How can Open Book Business Solutions benefits my business?

    We will look after both your bookkeeping & accounting needs. In the same time, we will take care of the reporting deadlines required by the ATO such as: BAS, IAS and Income Tax Returns. Therefore, you will have more time to focus on your business and clients.

  5. 5. How much does it cost to do my bookkeeping?

    Our bookkeeping rate is $50 plus GST per hour. The total costs depends on how much work need to be done. We can provide a free initial assessment on your bookkeeping needs with NO obligations.
